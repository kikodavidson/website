Project Overview
SaaS platform offering externships to college students, one of the most fiscally constrained audiences to sell to. Account had multiple campaigns running across different objectives. Budget was fragmented. Signals were diluted.
Project Execution
Consolidated the account into a unified evergreen purchase-focused framework. Layered interest testing, audience expansion, and creative testing. Built a dedicated bottom-funnel strategy with frequency segmentation for engaged users.

Within month one, CAC dropped over 70%. By month two, CAC was down ~90%. Trial signups jumped from 24 to 596, a 2,383% increase. Extern moved from fragmented experimentation to a controlled, scalable free trial engine.
