Core Beliefs

What I stand for.

Winning is everything.
This is a competitive field. I treat it like one. That means staying aggressive about improvement, disciplined in execution, and persistent in finding edges others miss, winning conversions, winning channels, and winning in the margins that compound over time.
Transparency Over Optics. Always.
Most agencies are optimized for retention, not outcomes. I take the opposite approach. You'll see what's working, what isn't, and why, in plain language, with full context.
Client Capital is My Capital.
I treat client capital the same way I treat my own. Allocation is deliberate, spend is justified, and nothing runs just to look busy. If dollars aren't producing, they're questioned, reworked, or pulled.
Stewardship Over Exploitation.
Exploitation shows up as over-spending, over-promising, and over-optimizing for the report instead of the business. Stewardship means restraint, clear tradeoffs, and decisions that leave the business stronger after the work, not dependent on it.
